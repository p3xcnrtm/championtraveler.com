Cost of a Trip to Cannaregio, IT & the Cheapest Time to Visit Cannaregio

The average price of a 7-day trip to Cannaregio is $1,722 for a solo traveler, $3,093 for a couple, and $5,798 for a family of 4. Cannaregio hotels range from $96 to $509 per night with an average of $145, while most vacation rentals will cost $180 to $480 per night for the entire home. Average worldwide flight costs to Marco Polo International Airport (VCE) are between $719 and $1,189 per person for economy flights and $2,256 to $3,731 for first class. Depending on activities, we recommend budgeting $65 to $133 per person per day for transportation and enjoying local restaurants.

Traveling Cheap to Cannaregio

How cheap can you make a vacation to Cannaregio? The cheapest trip to Cannaregio is about $106 per person per day for travelers willing to take standby flights, deal with inconvenience, and otherwise limit travel expenses. About 1% of rentals are available in the $0 to $100 range for an entire place, and vacation rentals can be booked for as low as $20 per night. These inexpensive rentals must be booked as early as possible and may not be in the most desirable areas. 1-star hotels are more likely to be available, with rooms starting at around $83.
