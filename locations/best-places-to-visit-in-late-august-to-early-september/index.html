10 Best Places in late August to early September for Cheap Flights/Hotels, Nice Weather, and Small Crowds

Below are the best locations for a trip from August 27th to September 2nd. We’ve taken into account rain, temperature, travel advisories for safety, and number of tourists in the area. In late August to early September each of these places are the perfect balance between temperate weather and smaller crowds, which also translates into better deals for tourists.

Because we’ve located areas and times where there is a greater chance for hotels/flights to be under capacity and weather is likely to be temperate and dry, the best time to book these 10 trips is usually within 1-4 weeks before travel.

1. Buenos Aires, Argentina

Buenos Aires has perfect weather from August 27th to September 2nd. The weather is less than ideal in the weeks before and great in the weeks after. Tourism in the area is somewhat slow during this time. Choosing Buenos Aires this week is a great choice for anyone looking for nice weather and lower prices.

2. Portimao, Portugal

The weather in Portimao is excellent during this week with nice temperatures and low chances of rain. The month before is equally nice and the month after is just as perfectly temperate. Crowds are far below average in size, but it never gets too crazy. Go here in late August to early September if you want absolutely perfect weather and generally lower prices.

3. Telluride, CO, US

Due to the average number of tourists, a trip to Telluride in late August to early September tends to be neither the least nor the most expensive, but the weather couldn’t be better. This period of ideal weather comes after a long stretch of similarly-wonderful weather. If booking a longer trip, you’ll want to know that the weather will be slightly less enjoyable towards the end of 30 days. Due to the more remote nature crowds are usually manageable year-round.

4. Chianciano Terme, Italy

As with every place on this list, temperatures in Chianciano Terme feel excellent, rain is infrequent, and there are no travel advisories at the moment. Tourists looking for hotels and flights are very uncommon during this time, so prices may be at their lowest. Because it’s a little less common of a destination, massive crowds aren’t likely. We could also strongly recommend visiting earlier than this, and the weeks following have weather that is as good as late August to early September.
