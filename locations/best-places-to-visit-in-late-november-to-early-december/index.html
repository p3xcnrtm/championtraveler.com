10 Best Places in late November to early December for Cheap Flights/Hotels, Nice Weather, and Small Crowds

Below are the best locations for a trip from November 26th to December 2nd. We’ve taken into account rain, temperature, travel advisories for safety, and number of tourists in the area. In late November to early December each of these places are the perfect balance between temperate weather and smaller crowds, which also translates into better deals for tourists.

Because we’ve located areas and times where there is a greater chance for hotels/flights to be under capacity and weather is likely to be temperate and dry, the best time to book these 10 trips is usually within 1-4 weeks before travel.

1. Santorini, Greece

Santorini has perfect weather from November 26th to December 2nd. The weather is almost as nice in the weeks before and good in the weeks after. Tourism in the area is slow during this time. Choosing Santorini this week is a great choice for anyone looking for nice weather and lower prices.

2. Kowloon, Hong Kong

The weather in Kowloon is excellent during this week with nice temperatures and low chances of rain. The month before is nearly as nice and the month after is just as perfectly temperate. Crowds are far below average in size. Go here in late November to early December if you want absolutely perfect weather and generally lower prices.

3. San Diego, CA, US

Due to the lower number of tourists, a trip to San Diego in late November to early December tends to be much less expensive, and the weather couldn’t be better. This period of ideal weather comes after a long stretch of similarly-wonderful weather. If booking a longer trip, you’ll want to know that the weather will be almost as enjoyable for the next 30 days. Note that it can get busy here in other seasons.

4. Orlando, FL, US

As with every place on this list, temperatures in Orlando feel excellent, rain is infrequent, and there are no travel advisories at the moment. Tourists looking for hotels and flights are very uncommon during this time, so prices may be at their lowest. The many hotels here can fill up, but probably won’t during this period. We could also strongly recommend visiting earlier than this, and the weeks following have weather that is as good as late November to early December.

5. Protaras, Cyprus

Because Protaras can get busy, this is an excellent time to avoid crowds.With far fewer travelers booking during this time compared to the busiest months, hotels and airlines will probably offer some incentives to stay during this period. This is a good time to visit, but the weeks beforehand are also very nice. If you’re planning to stay for more than a week, you can expect slightly less-pleasant weather.
