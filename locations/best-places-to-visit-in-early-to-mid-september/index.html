10 Best Places in early to mid September for Cheap Flights/Hotels, Nice Weather, and Small Crowds

Below are the best locations for a trip from September 10th to September 16th. We’ve taken into account rain, temperature, travel advisories for safety, and number of tourists in the area. In early to mid September each of these places are the perfect balance between temperate weather and smaller crowds, which also translates into better deals for tourists.

Because we’ve located areas and times where there is a greater chance for hotels/flights to be under capacity and weather is likely to be temperate and dry, the best time to book these 10 trips is usually within 1-4 weeks before travel.

1. Rome, Italy

Rome has perfect weather from September 10th to September 16th. The weather is less than ideal in the weeks before and ideal in the weeks after. Tourism in the area is somewhat slow during this time. Choosing Rome this week is a great choice for anyone looking for nice weather and lower prices.

2. Barcelona, Spain

The weather in Barcelona is excellent during this week with nice temperatures and low chances of rain. The month before is not quite as nice and the month after is slightly less temperate. Crowds are below average in size. Go here in early to mid September if you want absolutely perfect weather and generally lower prices.

3. Berlin, Germany

Due to the average number of tourists, a trip to Berlin in early to mid September tends to be neither the least nor the most expensive, but the weather couldn’t be better. This period of ideal weather comes after a long stretch of similarly-wonderful weather. If booking a longer trip, you’ll want to know that the weather will be slightly less enjoyable towards the end of 30 days. Note that it can get busy here in other seasons.

4. Sydney, Australia

As with every place on this list, temperatures in Sydney feel excellent, rain is infrequent, and there are no travel advisories at the moment. Tourists looking for hotels and flights are uncommon during this time, so prices may be lower. Because it’s a little less common of a destination, massive crowds aren’t likely. We could also recommend visiting earlier than this, and the weeks following have weather that is almost as good as early to mid September.
