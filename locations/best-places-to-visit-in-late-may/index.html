10 Best Places in late May for Cheap Flights/Hotels, Nice Weather, and Small Crowds

Below are the best locations for a trip from May 21st to May 27th. We’ve taken into account rain, temperature, travel advisories for safety, and number of tourists in the area. In late May each of these places are the perfect balance between temperate weather and smaller crowds, which also translates into better deals for tourists.

Because we’ve located areas and times where there is a greater chance for hotels/flights to be under capacity and weather is likely to be temperate and dry, the best time to book these 10 trips is usually within 1-4 weeks before travel.

1. Prague, Czechia

Prague has perfect weather from May 21st to May 27th. The weather is almost as nice in the weeks before and ideal in the weeks after. Tourism in the area is somewhat slow during this time. Choosing Prague this week is a great choice for anyone looking for nice weather and lower prices.

2. Rome, Italy

The weather in Rome is excellent during this week with nice temperatures and low chances of rain. The month before is equally nice and the month after is slightly less temperate. Crowds are above average in size. Go here in late May if you want absolutely perfect weather and don’t mind moderately-sized crowds.

3. Seville, Spain

Due to the lower number of tourists, a trip to Seville in late May tends to be much less expensive, and the weather couldn’t be better. This period of ideal weather comes after some similarly enjoyable weather. If booking a longer trip, you’ll want to know that the weather will be slightly less enjoyable towards the end of 30 days. Note that it can get busy here in other seasons.

4. Karlsruhe, Germany

As with every place on this list, temperatures in Karlsruhe feel excellent, rain is infrequent, and there are no travel advisories at the moment. Tourists looking for hotels and flights are very uncommon during this time, so prices may be at their lowest. Because it’s a little less common of a destination, massive crowds aren’t likely. We could also recommend visiting earlier than this, and the weeks following have weather that is as good as late May.

With far fewer travelers booking during this time compared to the busiest months, hotels and airlines will probably offer some incentives to stay during this period. This is a good time to visit, but the weeks beforehand are also very nice. If you’re planning to stay for more than a week, you can expect the weather to get much less pleasant.
